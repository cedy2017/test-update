Sotheby’s International Realty ®

Founded on the same commitment to exceptional service that characterized the Sotheby’s Auction House for more than two centuries, the Sotheby’s International Realty® brand is created and becomes known around the world for the distinctive properties it represents.

The Sotheby’s international Realty® brand is synonymous with excellence, luxury, taste and discernment, offering only the best and never settling for less. Sotheby’s International realty network provides access to luxury real estate and homes for sale worldwide, and the website allows you to search property listings globally, and includes a large inventory of luxury homes that matches your lifestyle choices such as waterfront, golf, island, vacation, mountain and many others.

List Group

The List Company was established in 1991, immediately after the real estate bubble burst in Japan. The focus of our organization was to transform the real estate industry that was in a period of chaos at the time, and restore the real value of real estate and trust in real estate as an asset.

Starting with real estate brokerage and marketing business, we have expanded the range of our business one step at a time from the sales of high quality original brand condominium and detached houses to town development business, during which time we have acquired an extensive track record and a wealth of unique know-hows.

We will continue to strive to be a company that is well-acquainted with a wide range of real estate transactions and that customers can rely on us as a one-stop “real estate specialist” for all types of real estate needs.

Creating new possibilities in real estate As a specialist that is well-acquainted with all types of real-estate businesses and a generalist that has comprehensive capabilities related to real estate, we also work hard for a real estate stock business, real estate flow business such as dwelling for sale and the sales for individuals and corporates as well as for domestic and abroad.

List Group consistently deals in every field of real-estate business incorporating the comprehensive strengths of “Sotheby’s International Realty®” of a worldwide network which combines the actual achievement and the trust we have developed so far in property project. We always make an effort to create new possibilities for regarding real-estate as our keywords “flow, stock and cross borders”.

Adding to the List Group family, List Sotheby’s International Realty is proud to announce its new office opening in Hong Kong, beside offices in Japan, Hawaii, Philippines and Singapore.
